Privacy Policy for NowSounds

Effective date: March 11, 2026

NowSounds ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the NowSounds mobile application collects, uses, and shares information.

1. Information We Collect

Microphone Access
NowSounds requests access to your device microphone so the app can detect pitch and provide guitar tuning functionality.

Audio Data
Audio picked up by the microphone is used to analyze pitch for tuning features inside the app. Audio is processed on-device and is not stored or uploaded by us.

In-App Purchase Information
NowSounds offers a one-time purchase to remove ads. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play or Apple. We do not receive your full payment card information.

Advertising Data
NowSounds uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device and usage information to provide, personalize, and measure ads in accordance with Google’s policies.

Local App Data
NowSounds may store limited app state locally on your device, such as whether ads have been removed after purchase and other app preferences.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide guitar tuning and related app features
  • Process and restore the remove-ads purchase
  • Display ads in the free version of the app
  • Maintain app functionality and performance

3. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers that support app functionality, including:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Google Play Billing and Apple in-app purchase services for purchase processing

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

Microphone audio is not stored by us.

Locally stored app preferences and purchase status may remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.

Third-party providers may retain data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Choices

  • Deny microphone permission, although tuning features may not work properly
  • Disable or limit ad personalization through your device or Google settings where available
  • Uninstall the app at any time

6. Children’s Privacy

NowSounds is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Third-Party Services

NowSounds may use third-party services, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit

Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
